Terms and Conditions

The information below sets out the terms and conditions under which students are currently enrolled at Serviam Girls College. The Council retains the right to alter these from time to time. Any queries on these terms and conditions may be discussed with the Principal or the Business Manager.

Application Fee

A fee of $110 is payable for each application to cover registration costs. It is not refundable.

Payment of Fees

  • 01A schedule of fees is produced annually. All fees are due and payable on the dates specified
  • 02The payment of one term’s fees in advance applies to all new students entering the College. In normal circumstances this fee is payable in September prior to commencement. For enrolments finalised after September, the fee is payable immediately.
  • 03The College offers payment of fees by BPAY, direct debit, cheque, credit card or cash.
  • 04The Principal reserves the right to exclude a student from the College while any portion of the fees remains unpaid.
  • 05A pro-rata charge is made for new students entering the College for the first time, after a term has commenced.
  • 06Parent/Guardian and Student Interview with Members of the Admissions Committee
  • 07If parents temporarily withdraw a pupil for one term or more, a holding fee is normally applied at the discretion of the Principal.

Privacy Policy
